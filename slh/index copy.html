03 // QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate?

Students, freelancers, and software professionals under the age of 30 are welcome to join. Teams must have between 2 to 4 members.

Are there any registration fees?

No. Registration is entirely free. High-speed Wi-Fi, workspaces, meals, and event merchandise will be provided to all shortlisted teams.

Why do we need the team template file?

The template ensures all team configurations, chosen track details, and basic project frameworks are cleanly documented. You will need to fill out this document and upload it during registration.
