04 // QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the evaluation system managed?

Teams construct prototypes remotely based on their track selection. Your system must be brought down live to the 1-day evaluation center for direct scoring.

When are final data results announced?

All interactive pitch presentations conclude within a single daytime window. After a brief closed panel break, the ranking data is verified and announced live at the venue that same evening.

Is there an entry or registration processing fee?

No. The registration and intake pipelines maintain a strict zero-fee operation model. Local space utilities, power routing hubs, network nodes, and catering profiles are fully covered for all accepted teams.
