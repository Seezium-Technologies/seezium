03 // INQUIRY RESIDUE

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the online building format work?

Teams prepare and build their prototypes remotely. You will only arrive physically for the 1-day screening round to demonstrate your execution live to the panel.

When will final evaluations and results take place?

The entire presentation and screening process concludes within a single day. After a brief panel break, results are compiled and announced live at the venue on that same evening.

Are there strict requirements for the team composition?

Yes. All participating team instances must confirm a size between 4 to 6 members. Ensure your details are accurately mapped inside the downloadable template file before uploading.
